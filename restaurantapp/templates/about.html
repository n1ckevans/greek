Our Story

We welcome you to “a little piece of Greece”. Nick's Greek Café has grown from its humble beginnings as a deli into a full-service, family restaurant. We provide fresh, delicious meals in a fun, relaxed atmosphere. We follow authentic Greek recipes, and use ingredients imported from Greece.

We also pride ourselves on using locally sourced produce, dairy, and seafood. We also offer vegan, vegetarian and traditional Greek entrées as well as pastries, breads, and desserts.

We invite you to experience a taste of Greece with us!
